Privacy policy

Extension: X: hide follower count

Last updated: 2026-04-24

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. It has no accounts, no analytics, no remote servers, and no third‑party SDKs.

It only runs on x.com and twitter.com in your browser. A content script changes how follower and following numbers are shown on the page (replacing the visible number with a symbol) so you can focus on content instead of the count. All of this happens locally in the page; nothing is sent to the extension developer.

Permissions are limited to what is required to run that script on those sites. The extension does not read your DMs, passwords, or posts in any special way beyond normal page content available to client-side code on the page.

Changes to this policy will be posted on this page. The version shipped with the extension may point here or to the same content hosted in the project repository.
